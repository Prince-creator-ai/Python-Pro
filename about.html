About This Project

This project is built using Flask and displays sales data.

Definition of Each Variable:

  • OrderID: A unique identifier for each sales transaction.
  • CustomerName: The name of the customer who made the purchase.
  • Product: The name of the product sold.
  • Category: The category or type of product (e.g., Electronics, Clothing).
  • Quantity: The number of units sold in the transaction.
  • Price: The price per unit of the product at the time of sale.
  • Date: The date when the sale occurred.
  • Region: The geographical region where the sale was made.
